Understanding the Hero Compass

The Hero Compass expands four core archetypes (Pirate, Knight, Cowboy, Wizard) into 16 distinct heroic personalities. Each main quadrant represents a blend of two primary archetypes, with four gradations showing different intensities of those traits.

The Pure Types (corner positions) represent maximum expression of both traits, while Balanced Types show moderate levels of each characteristic. The Hero Compass provides a nuanced way to understand personality that goes beyond simple categories to capture the full spectrum of heroic behavior and motivation.

Each profile includes Core Traits (fundamental characteristics), Approach (how they handle situations), and Strengths (natural advantages), providing a comprehensive view of each heroic archetype focused on positive attributes and capabilities. Every person is the hero of their own story - the Hero Compass simply helps you understand what kind of hero you are.
